Facts About Dogs

Dogs have a sense of time. Its been proven that they know the difference between a hour and five. If conditioned to they can predict future events, such as regular walk times.

A study was conducted on dogs being away from their owners for varying hours and studies show that dogs who were away from their owners the longest showed greatest amount of affection!
